Our Story

Founded in 2007, 2MB Computer started with a simple mission: to make quality technology accessible to everyone while contributing to a more sustainable future. What began as a small repair shop has grown into one of India's most trusted destinations for refurbished electronics.


Over the years, we've served thousands of happy customers, helping them save money without compromising on quality. Every device that passes through our hands undergoes rigorous testing and certification to ensure it meets our high standards.
